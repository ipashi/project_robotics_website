Lakshmanaraksha (The line Follower Robot)

“There are two different paths and I took the less travelled path and which has made all the difference” LAKSHMANREKHA is a line following event in which the line shall be black on white background The robot should complete the whole path in less time. The robot has to traverse a path, a path which comes with deviations, distractions and cross paths. A robot which can stick to its path without going off track will be the champ of the event. Being automated, they are usually very fast in traversing the path and the fastest will definitely win the title.
